About Me

I’m Caio Guedes, a software engineer based in Belo Horizonte, MG, Brazil.

I have been in the games industry as a programmer since 2020. My main areas of interest are computer graphics, low level programming and game engine development, which are themes of most of my studies and personal projects nowadays.

I have graduated with a bachelor’s degree in Computer Science from Universidade Federal de Minas Gerais (UFMG).

Currently I work as a programmer for Manifesto Game Studio.
